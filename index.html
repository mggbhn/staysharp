What We Do!

Stay Sharp is a website that helps the everyday person understand why living sustainably is important for both our earth and their life. We offer tips and small changes that everyone can make in their everyday lives that have big imapcts on our environment in order to assist beginners at the start of their sustainability jounrey. And for both those who already live a sustainable lifestyle or are just getting into it, we encourage everyone to maintain their sustainable lifestyle with a fun points rewards system! We know that it is important to educate our community about the importance of sustainability because if they do not know why it is important to live sustainably, then they will never be able to live a sustainable lifestyle, and we encourage our users to do so as well.
